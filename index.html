How It Works

1. Select Your Quiz

Choose your desired category, difficulty, and number of questions. Your quiz is fully customizable to suit your needs.

2. Start Answering

Challenge yourself with timed questions. Track your progress with our live progress bar and see how you're performing on the fly.

3. Track Your Progress

About Us

Our Mission

At Quiz Mee, we believe that learning should be engaging and effective. Our platform is designed to help you master new topics and reinforce existing knowledge through powerful, customizable quizzes. We're committed to building a tool that makes studying simple, smart, and fun.

Sleek, Intuitive Design

We've built Quiz Mee from the ground up with a clean and modern user interface. Our smooth design ensures you can focus on the questions without any distractions. The interface is responsive and works flawlessly on any device, from your phone to your desktop.

Personalized Performance Tracking

Go beyond just getting a score. With our built-in analytics, you can track your progress, review past attempts, and see your best scores. The local leaderboard gives you a quick snapshot of your performance history, helping you see where you've improved and where you can focus next.

Your Privacy, Our Priority

Your data is important, and we treat it with respect. By default, all your quiz scores and history are stored securely on your own device using local storage. This means your personal information remains private, giving you full control.

Our Features

For Learners

Take on timed quizzes in your favorite subjects. Customize your experience by choosing categories and difficulty levels. Get instant feedback on your answers and a detailed summary of your performance.

For Educators

Create a personalized learning experience for your students. With advanced options for categories, question count, and difficulty, you can generate practice quizzes tailored to your curriculum.

Comprehensive Analytics

Dive into your performance data with ease. Our dashboard provides clear insights into your quiz history, including your best attempts, accuracy rates, and overall progress. This helps you study more effectively.
